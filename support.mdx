## Contact

- Get an API key: `https://dashboard.yourdomain.com`
- Support email: `support@yourdomain.com`

## When requesting help

Include:

- `batch_id`
- `request_id` header (if returned)
- Timestamp of the request
- Steps to reproduce and sample filenames (no sensitive images)

## Operations

- Expected processing time: minutes for small batches; scales linearly with image count.
- Status page: `https://status.yourdomain.com`
